About Us

A Catholic Non-Profit making and Humanitarian Organization

Uromi JDPCI is a Faith-based Non-Governmental Organization borne out of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi with a mandate to promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development and to provide succor for the less privileged. Our activities cover the geographical area of the Catholic Diocese of Uromi which comprises the 5 Local Government Areas in Edo Central Senatorial district.

OUr Mission

To promote integral human development through motivation and participatory actions with relevant stakeholders toward policy reforms, empowerment of the people, and enhancement of human dignity for meaningful living.

A just and developed society where people coexist in peace.

  1. Integrity: Committed to doing the right thing at the right time
  2. Accoun ability: We hold ourselves responsible to all stakeholders
  3. Transparency: We demonstrate openness, honesty and welcome constructive criticism
  4. Excellence: We do ordinary things in extraordinary ways
  5. Team Spirit: We uphold friendly dis position and oneness towards achieving our goal

Uromi JDPCI is premised on the mandate of God to humankind which is to take care of His creation, to be on the side of the poor, to live in peace with all people, and to preserve human dignity and the common good of all.
